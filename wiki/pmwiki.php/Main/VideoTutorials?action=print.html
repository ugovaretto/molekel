Main: VideoTutorials

High resolution short video tutorials in Flash format. Click on the images to start the video in a new window.

Electron density

Shows how to generate an electron density surface color coded with Molecular Electrostatic Potential values.

Real-time update

Interactive generation of an electron density surface using the real-time update check-box to help in optimally adjusting the bounding box used for computation.

Molecular orbitals

HOMO generation.

Shaders

Shows how to apply transparent plastic and x-ray effects to an electron density surface.

High resolution export & vibrational modes

Generation of vibrational modes animations and still images, image enhancement through anti-aliasing and export to high resolution image through the use of the "Scaling" parameter in the save dialog box.

Radiation spectra

Visualization of radiation spectra and export to PDF.

Edit bonds

Remove and add bonds.
